FlowFit

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

FlowFit is built for fitness, wellness, and companion tracking. This policy explains the data the app may collect or process when you choose to use those features.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact support@flowfit.com.

Data You Provide

You may provide account information, profile details, age, body measurements, goals, units, Buddy companion settings, workout entries, mood or wellness notes, and other information you enter in the app.

Sensor and Device Data

With your permission, FlowFit may use heart-rate, motion, activity, location, camera, photo library, and notification permissions. These permissions support workout tracking, wellness missions, geofences, activity classification, optional camera/image features, and local reminders.

How FlowFit Uses Data

  • Authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • Sync profile, Buddy, workout, and heart-rate records.
  • Personalize goals, wellness missions, and workout history.
  • Classify activity and support heart-rate or sensor features.
  • Send local reminders and keep the app reliable.

Service Providers

FlowFit uses Supabase for authentication and database sync. Device APIs and SDKs such as Samsung Health Sensor API, geolocation, camera, image picker, local notifications, and on-device ML packages may process data needed for the features you choose to use.

Security and Retention

Data sent to Supabase is transmitted over HTTPS and protected by database access controls. Local data may remain on your device until you remove it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app. The in-app deletion flow also attempts to clear local FlowFit account data on the current device. Some records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

Your Choices

  • You can deny optional permissions or turn them off in device settings.
  • You can review or update profile data in the app.
  • You can initiate in-app deletion from Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated app data from the public deletion page.

Policy Updates

This policy may change when FlowFit features, service providers, or legal requirements change. The latest version is posted on this page.
