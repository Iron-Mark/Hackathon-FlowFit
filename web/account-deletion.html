Account Deletion

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This page lets FlowFit users initiate deletion of their account and associated app data without reinstalling the app.

Request Deletion by Email

Send a deletion request from the email address used for your FlowFit account. Include the words "FlowFit account deletion request" and ask for deletion of your account and associated app data. You can use this page even if you cannot sign in or do not want to reinstall FlowFit.

Email deletion request

FlowFit may ask you to verify ownership of the account before completing deletion of the sign-in account.

In-App Deletion

  1. Open FlowFit and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  3. Read the warning, type DELETE, and confirm the request.

The in-app flow deletes app-owned profile, workout, Buddy, and heart-rate records from FlowFit servers, attempts to clear local FlowFit account data on the current device, requests deletion of the sign-in account, and signs you out.

What Is Deleted

  • FlowFit profile and onboarding records.
  • Buddy companion state.
  • Workout and heart-rate records stored by FlowFit.
  • Authentication account after backend/admin processing.

What May Be Retained

FlowFit may retain limited records where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute handling, or backup integrity. Retained records are not used to continue normal app personalization.

Timing

App-owned server records are removed when the in-app request succeeds. Email requests are processed after account ownership is verified. Deleting the sign-in account requires an authorized administrative process, so it may complete after the initial app-data deletion request.
